Gawker Media and ReadWriteWeb have hubs

Gawker Media and ReadWriteWeb have hubs

By Julien, on 29 Jan 2010

ReadWriteWeb is one of the most respected tech blogs in the Valley. Marshall, Frederic and Jared Smith, along with Erick and Steve at Techcrunch, have been early realtime supporters. ReadWriteWeb also wrote a very insightful report on this trend and you should read it.

Gawker Media is one of the biggest and most successful blog-oriented media companies. Their publications include the following names : Gawker.com, Defamer, Fleshbot, Deadspin, Lifehacker, Gizmodo, io9, Kotaku, Jalopnik and Jezebel.

Today, we’re quite proud to say, that they now push their content in realtime : they have their hubs : http://rww.superfeedr.com, http://gawker.superfeedr.com.

We’re very happy to have gawker and rww using Superfeedr. In the past we announced hubs for blog platforms, and we’re excited to see what will happen for another kind of publisher : media companies.

It’s been amazing to see how some implementers of the PubSubHubbub protocol already found their hubs and subscribed to several feeds1 :)

You (and your app) could also benefit from a superfeedr hosted hub if you do publish content on the web. Reach out!

1 To be fair… they almost forced us to write about these hubs!
